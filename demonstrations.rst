Demonstrations
=================

The flood-related mapping services, developed in the context of the CopernicusLAC EO Service, have been operationally tested through two demonstrations:

* A first demonstration in the Caribbean region, focusing on flood events in Jamaica, Guyana and Belize. 
* A second demonstration in Colombia.
